About

BohConf is a 1-day, multi-track, unconf-inspired conference dedicated to learning about the latest technologies and practices in the web and mobile design/development world.

Professionals and enthusiasts of all backgrounds are encouraged to attend, participate, and most importantly: get engaged.

In supporting this mission BohConf is inexpensive and accessible to everyone.

Follow @bohconf for the latest news.

Venue

BohConf may take place in the Mt Vernon neighborhood of Baltimore at 1030 N Charles St, Baltimore, MD 21201.

There are hotels in the area but we encourage you to stay for free with some locals.

Street parking is available in the neighborhood. You can also take the Circulator and other means of public transportation.
