About BohConf

Back to top

BohConf is the official RailsConf 2011 unconference. At BohConf, we're going to get our hands dirty writing code and sharing ideas in an open and free-form environment. This hacking-centric event will include community code drives featuring well known OSS authors, barcamp-style discussions, free wifi, free seating, a code retreat and a programming competition. It is free and will run alongside RailsConf in the convention center. Everyone is welcome at BohConf. Registration is free and can be completed at https://en.oreilly.com/rails2011/public/register

You can track updates and developments by following @bohconf on Twitter and by contributing to the BohConf wiki. Your input will shape this event.

When & Where

Back to top

BohConf 2011 will be held at the Baltimore Convention Center, May 16-19. O'Reilly Media, the producers of RailsConf, have been kind enough to give BohConf space in the convention center alongside RailsConf - right in the middle of action.

Sessions

Back to top

Community Code Drive

The Community Code Drive is a place to meet and work with the authors of many popular open source projects. Come to help hack on code, find and fix bugs, or just watch a seasoned developer at work. Check out the schedule to see which OSS authors are participating (and feel free to add yourself)!

Barcamp

A Barcamp is a "user generated conference", so there's structure without an agenda. Bring your own questions and your own answers, and we'll hold our own BohCamp.

Open Government Hackathon

Luigi Montanez of Sunlight Foundation will be organizing and running an Open Government Hackathon. Bring your laptop and be prepared to work on some open government datasets!

JRuby Code Drive

EngineYard's team of JRuby experts will be hosting a code drive on best practices for JRuby development. All people are welcome - regardless of your level of experience with JRuby.

Guided Exploration

Guided exploration sessions will last approximately 2 hours and will feature an expert in a given technology providing instruction on that technology. More details TBD.

KidsRuby

Ron Evans (@deadprogram) will be running a session for KidsRuby. Many students from Baltimore's own Digital Harbor High School will be in attendance and we DO need volunteers. To sign up as a volunteer please complete the volunteer sign up form. To sign up as an attendee please complete the attendee sign up form. Note that your kids are indeed more than welcome at this program!

What is Boh?

Back to top

The name BohConf is an homage to the Baltimore institution National Bohemian (or Natty Boh). Every town has their local light beer of choice, and ours is Natty Boh. Make sure to grab one (or several) when you're in town.

Ignite RailsConf 2011

Back to top

The people bringing you BohConf are also organizing Ignite RailsConf - the unofficial preparty for RailsConf. Ignite RailsConf 2011 will be taking place on Monday 5/16 at 6:00pm. Ignite will once again be held at the Sheraton Inner Harbor - one of the two official hotels for RailsConf.

What is Ignite? Fast-paced, fun, thought-provoking, social, local, global - Ignite is all of these and more. It's a high-energy evening of 5-minute talks by people who have an idea - and the guts to get on stage and share it with the rest of the Rails community.

Submit a speaking proposal today! Buy your tickets now. Make sure to follow @igniterailsconf for updates!
